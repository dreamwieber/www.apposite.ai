Bossa Nova Bounce: Balloon Frenzy!

A truly spatial-first game.

Everyone knows the rule: keep the balloons in the air!

A fun spin on the classic game of Keepy Uppy, Bossa Nova Bounce will get you moving and grooving while you try to keep the balloons from hitting the ground.

Spatial audio provides cues as the balloons inflate all around you, advanced hand-tracking combined with high-quality sound effects and ARKit combine to give you the sense that you're bopping around real balloons.

And with Game Center Leaderboard support, you can go for the global high score!
