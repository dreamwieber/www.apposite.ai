Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Overview

SageBrush / Apposite LLC ("we") do not collect any personal information, nor do we retain information specific to your artwork such as prompts or sketches beyond the time necessary to process your generated imagery, or to the extent required for basic functionality like authentication.

We also do not share your information with any 3rd parties, except to the extent necessary for basic app functionality like authentication.

Any and all personally identifiable information is anonymized wherever possible.

Authentication

To manage your account effectively, we may collect information necessary for authentication and account access.

Data Usage

We collect data essential for the operation of the App and improving user experience. We respect your privacy and handle all personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

This may include email address, display name, and phone number if using 2-factor authentication. The app generates and stores a unique identifier for user authentication purposes.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@apposite.ai
