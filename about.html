Made with love, in Oregon.

Apposite is the app design studio of Gregory Wieber, creator of Polychord for iPad. Gregory has been making apps since 2010, including nearly eight years at Google where he worked on creative prototypes for Google Search and other well known products.

Gregory formed Apposite with his wife and design partner Colleen Clery. Together they own and run Clery Farm, an 80 acre historic farm in the Pacific Northwest. The conservation work they've done there has inspired apps like Vibescape, and is the future site of Solar Punk School – an innovative, montessori-based learning center set in a preserved oak woodland.

Apposite's focus in on creative applications that explore the forefront of technology to create one-of-a-kind experiences - with a strong focus on immersive content for Apple Vision Pro. If you're looking for a creative, multidisciplinary studio to help with your next project, get in touch – limited availability, serious inquiries only.
