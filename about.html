The Studio

Made with love, in Oregon.

Apposite is Gregory Wieber and Colleen Clery. We've been building apps together for over 15 years — musical instruments, creative tools, immersive environments — crafting experiences that move people. Our work has been featured by Apple and loved by users around the world.

In 2020, we left Silicon Valley to set up shop in the heart of Oregon wine country, on an 80-acre historic farm we've been restoring through regenerative agriculture.
